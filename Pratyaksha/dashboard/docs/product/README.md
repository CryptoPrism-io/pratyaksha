## ICE Scoring Reference

| Score | Impact | Confidence | Ease |
|-------|--------|------------|------|
| 10 | Critical user need | Data-backed | < 1 hour |
| 7-9 | Significant improvement | Strong feedback | < 1 day |
| 4-6 | Nice to have | Assumption | 1-3 days |
| 1-3 | Minor enhancement | Speculative | > 1 week |

**ICE Score** = (Impact × Confidence × Ease) / 10
